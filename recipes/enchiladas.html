Enchiladas

Description

Enchiladas are delicious, nothing is quite like an enchilada. Enchiladas are the cornerstone of any healthy diet. These enchiladas are guranteed to be loved by the whole family.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • enchilada sauce
  • cheese
  • tortillas

  1. Step 1 : Preheat Oven
  2. Step 2 : Arrange tortillas in glass 9x11 pan
  3. Step 3 : Pour 1/2 cup of chicken and 1 tablespoon of sauce per enchilada
  4. Step 4 : Cover tortillas with sauce and cheese
  5. Step 5 : Bake at 400 for 30 minutes and enjoy
